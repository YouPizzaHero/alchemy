About

An apprentice's grimoire — bind the four primal elements within the Circle, and watch the world write itself in burning gold.

How to play

  • Drag elements from the library into the empty slots of the Circle of Binding.
  • Fill every slot — the channels ignite, race around the crucible, and a new element rises with its true name burning across the board.
  • At higher ranks the crucible glows when ready — tap it to fire smaller brews without filling every slot.
  • Click any tile in your library to open its lineage — what made it, what it makes.
  • Open the Tree to see every discovery branching from the four primal seeds.
  • Discoveries stay in your library forever. Clear empties the slots; Reset wipes all progress.

Progression

Eight ranks, four alchemical circles. As you rise, the inscribed figure on the board changes and your circle widens — from the twin lens of the Vesica Piscis, to the trinity of the Triquetra, to the four-fold Squaring of the Circle, and finally to the five-pointed Pentacle. Bigger circles unlock deeper recipes that no pair-chain can reach.

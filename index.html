About

An apprentice's grimoire — drag and combine the four elements of creation to unearth the world hidden inside them.

How to play

  • Drag any element from the library onto the workspace.
  • Drop one element onto another to combine them.
  • Discoveries are kept in your library forever.
  • Hover a tile and click the ✕ to remove it. Clear empties the workspace; Reset wipes all progress.

Progression

Eight ranks await — Initiate, Apprentice, Adept, Scholar, Mystic, Sage, Archmage, and finally Demiurge at 100% discovered.
